About Us

Get Started Easily With Our Academy

At Madadi Academy, we believe that language learning should be both effective and enjoyable. Our experienced and passionate instructors use the latest teaching methodologies and resources to ensure that you gain confidence and fluency in English. We offer a variety of courses, including grammar, conversation, business English, exam preparation, and more.
Our mission is to empower you with the language skills necessary to achieve your personal and professional goals. We are committed to creating a supportive and engaging learning environment where you can thrive.

Our Journey

  • 2019 - Founding of Madadi Academy

    Madadi English Learning Academy was envisioned with a mission to make quality English education accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or location.

  • 2020 - Foundation & Early Growth

    Officially founded in 2020, the academy opened its doors to the first batch of students. With a small team of passionate educators, we began offering basic English courses to local students.

  • 2021 - Expansion of Programs

    As demand grew, we expanded our curriculum to include intermediate and advanced courses. We also introduced specialized courses in business English and exam preparation (IELTS, TOEFL).

  • 2022 - Going Digital

    Embracing the power of technology, we launched our online learning platform, allowing students from across the globe to join our classes. This move was crucial, especially during the pandemic, ensuring uninterrupted learning.

  • 2023 - Community Outreach & Partnerships

    Our academy began collaborating with local and international organizations to provide scholarships and free courses to underserved communities. This year also marked the launch of our professional development programs for teachers.
